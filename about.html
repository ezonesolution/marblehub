Who We Are

Local Showroom, Practical Guidance, Long-Term Materials

Marble Hub started with a simple need – customers in and around Chiara and Burari wanted reliable marble, tiles, granite and sanitary ware under one roof instead of running to different shops and dealers for each item.

Today, we are a focused destination for stone and bathroom solutions for homes, apartments, independent houses, showrooms and commercial spaces across West Bengal. Our display is designed so you can actually see how materials will look together – marble with tiles, tiles with sanitary ware, granite with kitchen fittings.

We believe in practical, straight-forward advice. If a material is high-maintenance, we tell you that. If a more budget-friendly option will work just as well in your space, we suggest that too.

We are not just a marble shop or a tile shop – we are a planning partner for your floors, kitchens and bathrooms, helping you select materials that look good and work well in day-to-day life.

What You’ll Find at Marble Hub

  • Italian & Indian marble for floors, stairs, countertops and temples.
  • Wall and floor tiles for bathrooms, kitchens, living rooms and outdoors.
  • Granite for kitchen platforms, steps, exteriors and heavy-use areas.
  • Sanitary ware & bath fittings – basins, WCs, faucets, showers and accessories.

How We Help You Choose the Right Materials

Whether you’re a homeowner, contractor or interior designer, we follow a simple, clear process.

Step 1 · Understand Your Site

Share Plans, Photos or Basic Measurements

You can bring your architect drawing, rough sketch or even mobile photos. We ask where you want to use marble, tiles, granite and sanitary ware and what look you’re aiming for.

Step 2 · Shortlist Practical Options

Mix of Looks, Budget & Maintenance

We then suggest a few combinations of materials – for floors, walls, counters and bathrooms – explaining the pros and cons of each, in simple language.

Step 3 · Finalise & Deliver

Lock Lots & Coordinate Delivery

Once you’re comfortable with the selection, we finalise quantities, block lots where needed and plan dispatch so your contractor gets material on time.
