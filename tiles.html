How We Help You Pick the Right Tile Finish

Finishes and sizes make a big difference – we keep it simple and clear.

Finish 01 · Matte / Anti-Skid

Safe for Bathrooms & Balconies

Matte and anti-skid tiles reduce the chance of slipping in wet areas. We show you options that still look modern while staying practical for daily use.

Finish 02 · Glossy

Bright Walls & Easy Cleaning

Glossy wall tiles reflect more light and are easy to wipe clean – perfect for bathroom walls and kitchen backsplashes where stains are common.

Finish 03 · Textured / Stone-Look

Feature Walls & Outdoor Spaces

Textured tiles add character to TV walls, reception backdrops and exterior areas. We help you pick patterns that complement the rest of your design.
