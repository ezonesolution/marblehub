How We Help You Choose the Right Granite

From colour and thickness to finish – we simplify the decisions for you.

Step 1 · Usage & Budget

Understand Where Granite Will Be Used

We start with your layout – is the granite for kitchen counters, stairs, floors or outside? Based on this, we shortlist options that fit your budget and wear-and-tear needs.

Step 2 · Shade & Maintenance

Choose the Right Colour & Finish

Dark shades hide stains better, lighter shades make areas look bigger. We also explain polish vs leather / flamed finishes so maintenance stays realistic for your family or business.

Step 3 · Sizes & Delivery

Finalise Slab Sizes & Transport

Once you’re happy with the granite, we coordinate with your contractor on cutting sizes, edge profiles and delivery timing so the material reaches site on schedule.
